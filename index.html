Synthetic Biology & Bioinformatics

I like to think there is something fundamentally computational about biology; life is information. Genetics, epigenetics, genomics, metabolomics...these - and many more - areas are dealing with questions about complex information networks that computer science has been taking a look at for decades.

Most of my projects on synthetic biology and bioinformatics have either been crafted in conjunction with, or heavily inspired by, the iGEM Toronto synthetic biology research team.
