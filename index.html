Computational Chemistry

I have dipped my toes in many areas of computational chemistry. From exploring ab initio methods like DFT and Hartee-Fock to calculate many different properties, to more empirical methods such as molecular docking simulations, and even applying unsupervised Machine Learning to gain insight on molecular strcutures for Drug Discovery.

I've had opportunities to work on this awesome topic as part of University courses, in Hackathons, and most notably as a volunteer researcher at the drug discovery startup Gene2Lead!

  • A bit on Molecular Docking
  • Clustering Algorithms on different representations of molecular structures
